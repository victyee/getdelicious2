What is getdelicious

getdelicious is an online food truck directory. We list catering packages of food trucks Australia wide. All packages are updated by it's individual food truck operators

What sort of events do food trucks cater for?

Any! Our food trucks are happy to cater for any event. Popular choices includes weddings, birthday parties, corporate events, and all sorts of private events

Can I customise the food packages?

Absolutely! Our food truck operators welcomes your call and more than happy to work out a package that suits you
